AI For All Lecture Series

Project Description

AI for All Lecture Series is an event dedicated to making artificial intelligence more accessible and inclusive. As part of the Youth STEAM initiative, we are deeply committed to empowering young minds to explore the intersections of science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics. The primary goal of our AI for All Talks is to interview various professionals and gather their insights on AI in their respective fields, with a particular focus on educating and inspiring youth.

First Lecture Summary

On October 19th, 2024, AI For All Lecture co-leads invited Dr. Huang, who will discuss Digital Health Technology, AI, and the Future of Medicine. During this seminar, participants will learnt about various aspects of AI, including how health agencies keep us safe and how AI assists in the creation of new medicines.

Second Lecture Summary

On January 31, 2025, AI For All welcomed its second guest speaker, Dr. Steve Yang, an MIT PhD and former McKinsey & Co. consultant, to discuss the future of oncology in the era of AI. During the seminar, Dr. Yang explained how AI can be used to detect cancer and support hospitals in both major and minor surgeries. He also highlighted the differences between machine learning (ML) and artificial intelligence (AI).
