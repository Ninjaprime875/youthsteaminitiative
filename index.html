Who We Are

We are a group of students passionate about STEAM who want to contribute to our community! In this team, we emphasize collaboration, perseverance, dedication, and kindness. We use our free technological solutions to empower local nonprofits, public institutions, and town governments in the Boston area, hoping to make a meaningful impact and inspire the next generation of problem-solvers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support our community through the development of innovative computer science projects.
