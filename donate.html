Donate

Supporting the Lexington Youth STEAM team is an investment in the future of innovation and creativity. Donations will help us support us by providing essential resources like coding tools, software, more opportunities to learn, and mentorship that can let students learn all aspects of STEAM. By contributing, you are fostering a collaborative learning environment where the STEAM team can develop problem solving skills, help the community, work on real world projects, and prepare for college and future careers. The Lexington Youth Steam Team has already done many projects, but with your support, it will ensure that they have access to more opportunities to grow and make a lasting impact on the world.
