Donate

Supporting the Youth STEAM Initiative by helping us cover our operational costs is an investment in a future of innovation and creativity. Donations will help us by providing essential resources like coding tools, software, more learning opportunities, and mentorship that can let students learn all aspects of STEAM. By contributing, you will foster a collaborative learning environment where the STEAM team can develop problem-solving skills, help the community, work on real-world projects, and prepare for college and future careers. The Youth STEAM Initiative has already done many projects, and your support will ensure that we have access to more opportunities to grow and make a lasting impact on the world.


The Youth STEAM Initiative is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All donations to the team are personally tax deductible. You can donate on Zelle by either sending money to lexyouthsteam@gmail.com or scanning the Zelle QR code to the right, and you can also donate through a check by making it payable to Youth STEAM Initiative Corp and emailing an image of the check to lexyouthsteam@gmail.com.
